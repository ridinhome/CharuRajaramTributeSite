Charu Rajaram was the loving wife of Vikram Rajaram, and adored mother of Virat Rajaram and Kayam Rajaram. A proud Gujarati and Bombayite (she never said Mumbai), she was born in Bombay in 1947 at the Lying-in Hospital opposite the Babulnath Temple in Malabar Hill in Bombay, to Kantilal Manchashankar Vakil (KM Vakil) and Vasantilal Vakil.

Her parents were leaders of the community. Her father was a judge and her mother was a doctor, a rarity at the time for a woman. She was the younger sister of her beloved brother, Suvarna Vakil.

Charu by her own account had a wonderful childhood. She was mischievous, as you can see from her cheeky smile and the sparkle in her eye in the image at right.
